What is ProjectHub

A project hub is a tool for keeping track of the progress of a design projects. When we work with design projects, we have lot of discussion and documents flow through mail. We want to refer some discussion and its documents, we have to go through all list of mails. ProjectHub is place where you can clear time line of your projects, documents and discussion of the every single aspects happened in your design projects.
